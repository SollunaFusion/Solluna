Beacon Hill


Join us at Beacon Hill for SolLuna’s first event! Mark your calendars for a day of workshops, learning, community bonding and a night of dancing. Dance under the stars and in a newly renovated workshop. Show off your sparkles and expect biodegradable glitter. Glam wear strongly encouraged. Bring your tent if you’d like to dance right up until the moment your head hits the pillow.

Workshop 2: Elasticity & Flow (Robert & Lydie)

Borrowing concepts from Brazilian Zouk and other dances, we will explore using continuity of movement, acceleration and deacceleration, and stretch to make our dancing more flowing and juicy.

Workshop 3: Axis Practice (Shane & Bo)

This class will pull techniques from axis-focused dances like Tango and Kizomba, offering us ways to add precision, balance, and dynamic changes to our dancing. We'll use drill exercises as well as a fun & fancy movement pattern to work on our own internal axis control, and awareness of our partners' axes. Once we learn the rules of balancing, spinning, and turning on an axis, we'll learn to break those rules in true fusion fashion.

Workshop 5: Let The Rhythm Set You Free (Shane)

As Fusion dancers, we dance to music that offers a feast of different rhythms, but tend to get stuck emphasizing only a few of those rhythms in our dancing. In this collaborative class, we'll work on identifying and expressing unusual rhythms in the music. We'll learn to put multi-rhythms and poly-rhythms in our bodies, and we'll explore what happens when we apply rhythmic constraints to our dancing in order to boost the creativity of our partnership. Be prepared to get schvitzy.

FAQ

Can I buy my ticket at the door?

No, tickets are available by pre-registration only, and sales are limited to 50 people, so get yours now!

What should I bring?

Water bottle! Bug spray! Dance attire (including comfy shoes if you aren't into dancing barefoot in grass). A dish to share, if participating in dinner. A tent, if planning to camp.

Can I camp?

Yes! Feel free to bring a tent or sleep in your car, and plan to be out by 11am.

Do I need a partner?

No! Everyone is welcome to dance with everyone!

Can I bring my animal?

Sorry, no pets. Contact us with special requests (i.e., service animals).

Is there water on site?

Yes.

Is there internet/cell reception?

Cell service is fine, but there is no wifi on site.

May I bring alcoholic beverages?

Yes, but please abide by the law and be respectful. We ask that no glass containers are brought on site for the safety of our dancing feet.

Who organizes SolLuna?

SolLuna is Beth Butler, Robert Pehlman, Alix Wicker, & Lydie Costes.

How to contact us?

Email us at sollunafusion@gmail.com with any further questions.
