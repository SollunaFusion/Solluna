About

Solluna Fusion Dance was created by a collective of dancers in Durham, NC in early 2018. We seek to offer opportunities for social dancers across genres to connect, explore, play, and learn. We believe that dancing outside is a sublime experience, so all our events include an outdoor component. Please contact us if you have a venue for a future Solluna event.
